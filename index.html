'ZEALICON' is the Annual Techno-Culural Fest, organised by JSS Academy Of Technical Education, Noida. This year, ZEALICON turns 10, with more exciting events and loads of prizes to be given away. The fest is scheduled to be organised from 14th March 2018 - 17th March 2018. Further details will be added soon, keep visiting this page to stay updated on ZEALICON'18.
