Pokemon at 5, and then Beyblade at 5.30. Tom & Jerry, Looney tunes, Scooby Doo, Shaktiman…. That’s so Raven...Video games..

Do you yearn to live and bring all those times back ?

If the answer is Yes...then we are back with our Zeal… are you ?

JSS Academy of Technical Education, Noida presents to you the most enthusiastic annual Techno-Cultural fest in NCR- the “Zealicon”, turning 10 with the 90’s nostalgia. This time with a different aura and touchstone but an antiquated journey down to one’s memory lane.

Join us on this nostalgic journey from 14th March 2018 - 17th March 2018 with opportunity to be a part of some airing and zestful events. Promising, loads of fun, prizes and exposure to make this fest Bigger this time!
